I am working as a postdoc fellow in Department of Computing at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University since December 2017. I also earned my PhD degree in 2017 from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) in the Department of Computing (COMP). Before my PhD study, we receivded Master and Bechelor degress from Nanjing University of Posts and Tecommunications in 2010 and 2013, respectively. I am interested in designing and implementing efficient and practial systems for improving the system and network sercurity and I am focus on applying dynamic and static program analysis methodology to addressing the challenging security issues in system and networt. Currently, my reseach topics include mobile system security, IoT sercurity, and telemantics security.
